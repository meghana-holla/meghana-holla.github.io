Bio

Hello, I am Meghana Holla. I am a Master of Science Computer Science student at the Virginia Tech. I obtained my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Data Science from PES University, India.

My research interest is in Machine Learning (ML) with focus on Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP). Specifically, I am interested in problems in Natural Language Understanding (NLU), Natural Language Generation (NLG), and those at the intersection of vision and language domains. I am currently working with Dr. Ismini Lourentzou on Natural Language Grounding in Videos.

Prior to this, I worked as a Technology Associate at Morgan Stanley, India. My undergraduate thesis advisor was Dr. Ramamoorthy Srinath, under whom I worked on the research problem of dialog-driven face construction and modification.
